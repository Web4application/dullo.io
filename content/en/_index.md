<h2>Why dullo?</h2>

dullo is a new-era open-source RPC and microservice framework built for the
world of AI systems, distributed applications, and next-generation cloud
services. It seamlessly connects microservices across regions, data centers,
and edge devices with integrated support for load-balancing, tracing,
auto-healing, authentication, and encrypted transport.

Built for simplicity. Engineered for scale.
